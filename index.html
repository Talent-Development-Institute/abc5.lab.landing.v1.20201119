ABC5-Lab in Secondary Schools
ABC5-Lab is a programme brought to you by HKBCS (Hong Kong Blockchain Society), which prepares Hong Kong's secondary students for success in the 21st century. Our lab provides practical experiences in the new ABC's of IT innovation.
Programme Overview
Since 2017, we have trained 5,000+ university students and selected secondary-level students, with many finding success in their academic and professional careers. We work with all the major universities in Hong Kong and circa 100 universities worldwide. Now, we would like to offer all of Hong Kong’s secondary school students the same opportunities for success.

Are these programmes too challenging for secondary-level students to understand?

No, the programmes have all been tailored to a level that fits a secondary learner.

Is it necessary for the applicant to set up a physical lab at school?

The IT Innovation Lab initiative is not restricted to setting up a physical lab. The ABC5-lab is a virtual lab, meaning it comes as an installed software.

Is my school eligible for the application of the OGCIO backed IT Innovation Lab funding?

The applicant must be a publicly-funded secondary school, including government aided, caput secondary schools and secondary schools under the Direct Subsidy Scheme (DSS).

How much subsidies can my school get from OGCIO?

Your school is able to get up to 1,000,000 HKD.

Do we need to advance the cost of ABC5-Lab before we are granted the OGCIO funding?

No, you are able to get access to ABC5-Lab’s content before you've gotten the funding, if you choose to order our programme.
